The Brand

Made for
the moment

Sweetheart Cider is a lifestyle-driven beverage brand designed to create joyful, social, and memorable experiences. It's feminine, fun, and emotionally engaging, crafted not just as a drink, but as a moment to be shared.

Born from the Sweet Heart Brands family in Loveland, Colorado, Sweetheart fills a lane that most craft cider brands ignore: the social, lighthearted, emotionally driven drinking occasion. Girls' nights, patio moments, celebrations, and everyday indulgences.

It's an approachable, fun alternative to wine and seltzer. Flavor-forward, crushable, and designed for the kind of moments people actually post about.
